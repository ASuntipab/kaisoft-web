Privacy Policy - Kai Soft

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all Kai Soft applications and games, including but not limited to Line Logic AI Puzzle. If you have any questions, please contact us at conansadogawa@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1.1 Game Data

  • Game progress, scores, and achievements
  • Level completion status and statistics
  • Player rankings and leaderboard positions
  • Custom levels and user-created content

1.2 Account Information

  • Email address (if you sign in with Google)
  • Google Account profile information
  • Player username and display name

1.3 Device & Usage Information

  • Device type, OS version, and device model
  • App usage patterns and gameplay analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • App version and build information

1.4 Advertising Data

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Google Advertising ID on Android)
  • Ad impressions and interactions
  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent status (iOS only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress, display leaderboards, and enable multiplayer features
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand how players use our games and improve game design and performance
  • Bug Fixes & Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Personalized Advertising: To serve relevant ads based on your interests
  • User Support: To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Firebase Realtime Database & Firestore: Cloud-based Google servers (encrypted in transit and at rest)
  • Local Device Storage: On your device via SharedPreferences (encrypted by your device's security)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash logs and error reports

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Firebase

  • User authentication (Google Sign-In)
  • Real-time database storage
  • Crash reporting (Crashlytics)
  • Analytics

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

  • Personalized ad delivery
  • Ad performance analytics

Google AdMob Privacy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4.3 Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with your Google Account, your email and profile information are shared with us. You can revoke this access anytime from your Google Account settings.

5. In-App Purchases

  • Purchase records are stored on Apple App Store or Google Play Store servers
  • We do not directly store your payment information (handled by the app stores)
  • For refund requests, contact your respective app store

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our apps are designed for all ages. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete such information. Parents concerned about their child's data should contact us at conansadogawa@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

  • Game Progress: Stored as long as your account is active
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 30 days for bug fixing
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 26 months
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

8. Your Rights & Choices

8.1 Access & Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.

8.2 Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen in the app. You may also request deletion by emailing us.

8.3 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → App Tracking, and disable tracking for our app
  • Android: Go to Google Settings → Manage your Google Account → Data & privacy → Ad personalization, and turn off personalized ads

8.4 Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing emails. Push notifications (if enabled) can be disabled in your app settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our apps constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
